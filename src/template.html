Are you an introvert or an extrovert?

There are a lot of myths surrounding the concept of introverts versus extroverts — one of the main ones that it’s an either-or situation.

You’re either an extrovert or an introvert. End of story.

But reality is a bit more complicated.

Extroversion and introversion live on two opposite ends of a spectrum. The way you get and put out energy helps determine where you fall on this spectrum.

But you can fall anywhere on this spectrum, not necessarily at one end or the other.
